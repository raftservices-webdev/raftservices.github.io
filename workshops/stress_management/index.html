About the Workshop

What is this workshop about?

Stress is something we all experience — but many of us have never had the space to truly understand it, or the tools to work through it. This intimate, half-day workshop is designed to do exactly that.

In a warm, non-judgmental setting, we'll explore what stress really is, how it shows up in our bodies, our relationships, and our work — and most importantly, practical, evidence-based strategies to manage it more effectively.

This workshop is for you if you feel overwhelmed, burnt out, or stretched thin — or if you simply want to understand yourself better and build greater resilience for life's inevitable challenges.

🧠

Understand Stress

Learn what stress actually is, how it works in the body, and why we react the way we do.

🌊

🛠️

Connect & Heal
